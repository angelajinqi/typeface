Typeface Process

Inspiration

I was inspired by the works from Muirmcneil website where it has various line weights, colours, and interesting overlays applied to the typography.

For my own design I implemented colours contrasting each other to create shadow and the main shape. A repetitive shape I used is a rectangle with rounded corners.

Draft

The design evolved from being “static” to having a movement effect. The lines create a rhythmic movement feel and to strengthen this idea I skewed the letters and also switched the shadow and foreground colour to so the shadow appears to be darker.

A challenge I faced at first is positioning each shape without a grid and deciding where to use the lines to create movement. I removed vertical lines to eliminate any confusion and to have more consistency in the overall typography.

In terms of coding, I was able to simplify everything by just having box-shadow instead of two shapes for each element.
